# Demo Brand Design System

## Brand

Demo is a fictional productivity SaaS for creators and founders.

## Audience

Busy solo founders, creators, and operators who want practical systems without vague motivation.

## Voice

- Clear, direct, and useful.
- Specific examples over generic advice.
- Confident but not hype-heavy.
- Short headlines, dense body copy, strong takeaway.

## Carousel Style

- Use strong hook slides for the first slide.
- Use 2-3 slide formats for news, hot takes, and single-stat posts.
- Use 5-7 slide formats for tutorials, frameworks, and save-worthy guides.
- Highlight important words with `**orange**` and secondary words with `__cyan__`.

## Content Rules

- The hook must make the viewer want the next slide.
- Every body slide must contain a concrete idea, example, or instruction.
- Avoid filler like "in today's fast-paced world."
- End with a practical CTA when the format calls for it.
